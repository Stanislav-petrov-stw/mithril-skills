No thresholds

The frictionless part is the product.

Most crypto apps lose people at "add your API keys" and "fund on-chain." We removed both.

  • Sign in with email, Google or Apple — an embedded wallet, no seed phrase
  • A trade-only agent wallet is provisioned automatically — no API credentials, one signature
  • Fund with a card, or Venmo / PayPal / Cash App with no KYC
  • Agent keys are trade-only (can't withdraw); your funds stay in your wallet
